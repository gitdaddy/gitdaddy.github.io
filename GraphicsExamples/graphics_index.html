Star Fight

The star fight project was done with the help of THREE.js which is a WebGL framework. Keys include WASD - pitch, yaw and roll, Q and E - forward and backward, space - fire. Press 2 for two player mode which uses IJKL for pitch, yaw and roll, U and O - foward and backward. - Takes a second to load

Authors and Contributors: Daniel Marsden, Andrew Garver, Dane Christenson(Star Fight)
