The goal of the game is to guess the 4 digit number with the lowest number of gueses.
- Pico: You guessed a correct number in the right position.
- Fama: You guessed a correct number but is not in the right place.
You must use your pico fama score to figure out the number.

Rules: Numbers are all unique and from 1 - 9. Every guess must follow suit.
